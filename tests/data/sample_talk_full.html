The Power of Faith

By Elder Jeffrey R. Holland

Of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles

April 2024 General Conference

My dear brothers and sisters, I speak to you today about the fundamental principle of faith and its transformative power in our lives.

Faith is not merely belief; it is a principle of action and power1. When we exercise faith, we invite divine power into our lives.

As the Apostle Paul taught, "Faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen"2. This eternal truth guides us through life's challenges.

Faith in Action

True faith manifests itself through our actions. When we act in faith, we demonstrate our trust in God's plan for us.

I testify that faith in Jesus Christ has the power to heal, to comfort, and to guide us home to our Heavenly Father. In the name of Jesus Christ, amen.

Notes

  1. 1. See Hebrews 11:1; Alma 32:21.
  2. 2. Hebrews 11:1.
